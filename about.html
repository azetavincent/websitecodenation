Who we are


We are one of the world leading group of companies that provide various products and services for the better development of the present and future. We were founded in 1990 by multi billionaire Eng. Anthony Okolo. He had a vision to own a group of companies so he bought various companies that shared the same vision. The company startd solely on provide engineering services to various company, then expand its IT department into a major part of the company that provides IT services for other companies. 10 years later, we decide that this company will be able to provide all the neccessary services required by major companies and the general public such as Insurance services, Computing and networking.
